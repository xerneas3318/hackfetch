Two things to keep in mind when working on this repo.

## No em dashes

The character `—` (em dash) is banned everywhere: code, comments, docs, commit messages, PR descriptions. Same for the en dash `–`.

Use one of these instead:

- a regular hyphen `-` for compound words like `low-level`
- a colon `:` when you'd normally use an em dash to introduce something
- a semicolon `;` for compound sentences
- parentheses `(like this)` for parenthetical asides
- just splitting the sentence with a period

If you're rewriting text that had em dashes, don't just search and replace. Read it and pick the right punctuation for what the sentence is doing.
